Why lubricate your cube?

It's tedious and time-consuming but consider:

  • cube longevity
  • feels better
  • a fun time

The springs that hold the cube core together will strip the plastic over time. While it's not enough to actually bore a hole through the center pieces, it creates plastic dust and an extremely unpleasant noise.

What you'll need:

  • a cube to lube
  • screwdriver
  • the will to continue
  • silicone based lubricant

*Traxxas 50k and Lunar are silicone based lubricants with different viscosities.

PLEASE for the love of god DO NOT USE:

  • WD-40 or any silicone spray
  • PAM or any cooking spray
  • Vaseline
  • furniture polish
  • olive oil
  • corn oil
  • sesame oil
  • butter
  • margarine
  • I Can't Believe It's Not Butter!®
  • hand cream
  • body lotion

Lubricate responsibly!!!
